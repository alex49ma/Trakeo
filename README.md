## How to use the app:

Welcome to Trakeo! The tool to track your finances. Let's have a look into it!

What can be measured can be managed: Did you know that the simple act of writing down your expenses is the single most effective way to take control of your financial future? It’s not just about numbers; it’s about awareness. When you track where every penny goes, you stop wondering where your money went and start telling it where to go. Today, I’m going to show you Trakeo—the tool designed to make this habit effortless and insightful.

Welcome to your Trakeo Dashboard. This is your financial command center. Right away, you can see exactly how you're doing this month. Have you stayed within your budget? This progress bar gives you the answer in seconds. Below, the breakdown shows you exactly which categories are consuming your income, so there are no surprises. //You can add accounts to separate where your money is held in case you are dealing with more than one bank or financial source.

Adding a transaction shouldn't be a chore. With Trakeo, it takes seconds. Just enter the amount, pick a category, and you're done. Need to track a subscription or your rent? Just toggle 'Recurring', set the interval, and Trakeo will handle it for you automatically forever.

Moving from another tool or have a bank export? You can bulk import transactions directly via CSV. Plus, Trakeo supports multiple accounts—track your checking, savings, and cash separately but view them all in one unified dashboard.

Your finances are unique, and your categories should be too. Trakeo lets you fully customize your income and expense categories. You can create main categories, assign them colors that make sense to you, and even add subcategories for granular tracking. Whether it’s 'Hobbies', 'Travel', or 'Education', organize it your way. We can later edit all categories in the Analytics tab.

Data is power. The Analytics tab turns your daily entries into actionable insights. visualize your spending trends over time. Are you spending more on groceries this month? Is your income growing? Filter by weeks, months, or years to get the full picture of your financial health.

And we're building this for you. Have a suggestion? The Feedback button is right there. We listen to our users to make Trakeo better every day.

Take the first step towards financial clarity. privacy-focused, ad-free, and designed for you. Start tracking with Trakeo today.

## Getting Started

First, run the development server:

```bash
npm run dev
```

Run the inngest client as well:

```bash
npx inngest-cli@latest dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) with your browser to see the result.

This project uses [`next/font`](https://nextjs.org/docs/app/building-your-application/optimizing/fonts) to automatically optimize and load [Geist](https://vercel.com/font), a new font family for Vercel.
